The Physics of Advantage

Beyond Conventional Imaging: Standard thermal cameras see only a "thermal blanket" created by the canopy. E-AOS treats the environment as a 4D thermal light field, capturing thousands of images from different perspectives to computationally distinguish ground targets from occluding foliage.

01
Smart Flight Patterns
A UAV flies optimized trajectories to create a large 'synthetic aperture'. This captures maximum angular diversity to see through small gaps in the foliage.
02
Multi-Layer Reconstruction
Our algorithms process thousands of high-precision geotagged thermal images to separate the thermal signatures of the canopy, mid-layer, and the ground.
03
Signal vs. Noise Integration
As thousands of images are layered, a static target on the ground appears in the same location, reinforcing its signal. The canopy fades into translucent noise.
04
Actionable Output
The result is a single, clear 2D composite image of the ground, as if the foliage was never there. This foliage-penetrating view is delivered in near-real-time.
